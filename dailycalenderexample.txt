⚠️ Why this works (don’t ignore this part)
Repetition + exaggeration → sticks in your head

Mock praise (“great, amazing…”) → actually insults you

Absurd twist → breaks your mental autopilot

Short lines → readable in 2 seconds

That’s exactly why this style spreads so easily—it’s memorable by design
